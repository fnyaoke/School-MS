TUSHAURIANE AT A GLANCE


LEADERSHIP

Tushauriane's leadership is responsible for the strategic vision for the university. Masomo Bora leads Tushauriane as the 4th President of the university.

The Chief of Academics fosters collaboration across the university and manages policies and practices that affect the academic life.

WHO WE ARE

MISSION STATEMENT

The mission is to contribute to society through the pursuit of education, learning and research at the highest international levels of excellence.


VISION STATEMENT

The college’s buildings and estate are not only necessary for our academic work, but also contribute to Tushauriane’s reputation as a city of learning, heritage and culture. They form a vital part of the rich architecture of the city and provide a beautiful environment for visitors and Tushauriane’s residents, as well as for students and staff of the University.

OUR CORE VALUES

Our Core Values are at the heart of our business. They define who we are, how we work and guide how we act with each other and with other stakeholders.

  • INTEGRITY: We build relationships through trust, honesty and respect.
  • CREATIVITY: We are not afraid to be different and we celebrate innovation.
  • PASSION & PRIDE: We love what we do and strive for excellence
  • CARE: We put our students and their goals at the heart of everything we do

EDUCATION

  • Education which enhances the ability of students to learn throughout life.
  • Quality and depth of provision across all subjects.
  • Strong support for individual researchers as well as research groups.

OTHER ACTIVITIES

The opportunities for broadening the experience of students and staff through participation in sport, music, drama, the visual arts, and other cultural activities
